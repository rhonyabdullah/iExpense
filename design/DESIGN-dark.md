## Brand & Style

This design system is engineered for high-performance financial tracking in low-light environments. The aesthetic is rooted in **Corporate / Modern** principles, adhering strictly to Material Design 3 (M3) dark theme logic.

The brand personality is precise, utilitarian, and focused. By utilizing a desaturated pastel palette against a deep neutral base, the UI minimizes eye strain while maintaining a sophisticated, high-contrast hierarchy. The emotional response is one of control and clarity—essential for managing personal or business finances. The interface feels expansive and immersive, utilizing depth through color luminance rather than traditional heavy shadows.

## Colors

The color palette leverages the M3 dark spectrum to ensure accessibility and vibrant contrast.

- **Primary (#D0BCFF):** Used for key actions and active states. Its desaturated nature prevents "glowing" or vibrating against the dark background.
- **Secondary & Tertiary:** Provide semantic variety for expense categorization (e.g., distinguishing between "Needs" and "Wants").
- **Surface Strategy:** This design system utilizes a tiered surface approach. As elements rise in elevation, they receive a higher luminance "Surface Container" value.
- **On-Surface (#E6E1E5):** A high-legibility off-white that reduces the harshness of pure white text while maintaining a high contrast ratio against the #1C1B1F background.

## Typography

This design system utilizes **Manrope** for display and headline roles to provide a modern, geometric personality that feels refined and high-tech. **Inter** is used for all body and label roles due to its exceptional legibility at small sizes, which is critical for financial data and line-item auditing.

- **Headlines:** Set in bold weights to anchor the page and provide immediate context for account balances.
- **Body Text:** Uses a generous line-height to ensure that dense financial data remains readable.
- **Labels:** Utilized for secondary metadata like timestamps, categories, and micro-copy.
- **Numerical Data:** For expense amounts, use `title-lg` or `headline-lg` to ensure financial figures are the primary focal point of the screen.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout follows a strict 8px grid system to ensure vertical rhythm and visual consistency.

- **Grid Model:** A **Fluid Grid** approach is used. On mobile devices, a 4-column grid with 16px side margins is standard. For tablet and desktop, the system scales to a 12-column grid.
- **Spacing Logic:** Components are separated by `md` (16px) or `lg` (24px) units to create "breathing room" in dark mode, preventing the UI from feeling cramped or cluttered.
- **Touch Targets:** All interactive elements maintain a minimum 48x48px hit area, aligned with the 8px grid.

## Elevation & Depth

In this design system, depth is communicated through **Tonal Layers** rather than shadows. This is a core M3 Dark principle.

1.  **Level 0 (Background):** Pure `#1C1B1F`.
2.  **Level 1 (Cards/Lists):** A subtle primary color overlay (approx 5-8% opacity) is applied to the surface, creating a slightly lighter "Surface Container" look.
3.  **Level 2 (Modals/Menus):** Further increased luminance.
4.  **Interaction Shadows:** Only used for floating action buttons (FAB). Use a soft, high-spread shadow with 0.3 opacity to avoid "harsh" edges against the dark background.

This approach ensures that the hierarchy is perceptible through color value shifts, which is more effective on OLED screens.

## Shapes

The shape language is **Rounded**, following the modern M3 standard.

- **Small Components (Buttons, Inputs):** 8px (`0.5rem`) corner radius.
- **Medium Components (Cards, Dialogs):** 16px (`1rem`) corner radius.
- **Large Components (Bottom Sheets):** 24px (`1.5rem`) or 28px corner radius on top edges.
- **Full Rounding:** Used for Chips and Floating Action Buttons to denote high interactivity.

This "Soft" approach balances the technical nature of a financial app with an approachable, modern feel.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid `#D0BCFF` background with `#382767` text.
- **Secondary:** Outlined with a 1px border of `#938F99`.
- **States:** Focused states should use a 12% primary color overlay.

### Input Fields
- **Style:** Filled style with a bottom indicator line.
- **Background:** `Surface Container High`.
- **Text:** `On-Surface` for input, `Secondary` for labels/placeholders.

### Transaction Lists
- **Item Height:** Minimum 72px.
- **Separators:** Use a low-opacity `Outline Variant` (#49454F) to subtly divide transactions without breaking the visual flow.

### Expense Cards
- **Background:** `Surface Container`.
- **Padding:** 16px internally.
- **Content:** Use `Tertiary` for "Spend" values and `Primary` for "Income" values to provide instant financial orientation.

### Chips
- **Usage:** Filtering categories (e.g., "Food", "Travel").
- **Style:** Outlined or Tonal (Secondary Container) based on selection state.
